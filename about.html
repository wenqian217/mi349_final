Company Profile:

Poli was established in 2005 and is headquartered in Songjiang, Shanghai with 3 subsidiaries. Over the years, the company has been committed to providing high-end pet food production services and technical services, and has provided product technology innovation and production services support for more than 300 brands around the world. As one of the top ten pet food enterprises in China, the company has a number of high-end professional pet food production lines, product quality has been recognized by the majority of customers, and won the honor of China's export enterprise "China quality integrity" unit. Since 2014, it has been jointly recognized as a high-tech enterprise by Shanghai Municipal Science and Technology Commission, Shanghai Municipal Finance Bureau and Shanghai Taxation Bureau of the State Administration of Taxation. We adhering to the "innovation, high efficiency, integrity and pragmatic" service tenet, "take the customer demand as the center" sincerely for the general customers to provide high standard of professional service, help customers solve the pet food technology development, production, quality, after-sales service, make more partners to share the achievements of our technology and information resources.
